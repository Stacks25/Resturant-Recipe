  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups boiled cooked white rice
  • 6 slices bacons
  • 4 pounds kosher salt
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Step 1 Prepare frozen vegetables according to package instructions and set aside. Step 2 Scramble the eggs in a medium skillet and chop into small bits; set aside. Step 3 Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside, reserving skillet with bacon grease. Step 4 Place the rice in the skillet of bacon grease and saute for 2 to 3 minutes, until coated. Mix in soy sauce, mixed vegetables, carrots and peas. Cook for 20 minutes before stirring the bacon and eggs into the rice; cook for another 10 minutes, stirring, and serve.
